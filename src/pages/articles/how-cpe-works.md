# How Cost-Per-Event (CPE) Works on ReadyBread

CPE is one of the most popular ways to earn on ReadyBread. It simply means you earn money for completing specific events inside a game. It actually is an acronym, meaning "Cost Per Event". It is really how all offerwalls and GPT (Get Paid To) platforms work.

## What Counts as an Event?  
- Reaching a certain level  
- Completing a tutorial  
- Unlocking a building  
- Finishing a chapter  
- Whatever is listed!

## Why CPE is Great for Users  
You can earn at your own pace, choose games you enjoy, and get rewarded as you progress naturally.

## Why We Use It  
CPE is one of the most transparent earning methods. You see the payout upfront and only complete tasks that interest you. It also creates a safe situation for our partners, avoiding risky download costs and upfront payments. Two things we never charge our users or partners for.
